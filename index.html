What's New

  • Online Classes on Microsoft Teams app

    Microsoft Teams is an application developed for student's daily classes in school. It helps to maintain the accurate record and generate summarized student attendance reports . This application reduces nervousness among students, as many are able to communicate more through online education than regular classes. This app is also collaboration app that helps your friends stay organized and have conversations all in one place.

    •          

  • Connected Classroom app for Assignments and Homeworks

    Connected Classroom is a free blended learning platform developed by CRISP educational organisation for schools that aim to simplify creating, distributing, and grading assignments and homeworks.This app allows you to see documents and links that teachers share with you.This is an education-friendly platform that brings the benefits of paperless sharing of homeworks and assignments.
